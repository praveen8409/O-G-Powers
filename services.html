Our Core Solar Solutions

On-Grid Solar Systems

Connected directly to your local government grid lines (SBPDCL/NBPDCL/JBVNL). Includes a bidirectional net-meter that routes extra electricity back to the grid to reduce or zero out your monthly bill. Ideal for retail shops, offices, and urban homes.
